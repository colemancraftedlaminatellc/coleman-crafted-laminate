What We Do

Installation Services

🪵

LVP / Luxury Vinyl Plank

Waterproof, durable, and built for real life. Perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and basements. Floats or glues down — we do it right either way.

📐

Laminate Flooring

Clean, sharp installs with proper expansion gaps, transitions, and trim work. No shortcuts, no squeaks, no callbacks.

🌲

Hardwood Installation

Nail-down, staple-down, or glue-down. We work with solid and engineered hardwood and get the subfloor right before we ever lay a plank.

🔨

Flooring Removal

Old carpet, tile, or existing flooring removed and hauled. Clean slate before the new floor goes in.

🪜

Trim & Transitions

Quarter round, shoe molding, T-moldings, reducers, and threshold transitions — all included, all installed clean.

About Us

Built on Craft. Run on Trust.

Coleman Crafted Laminate LLC is a family flooring installation business based in South Lyon, Michigan. Owner James Coleman brings over 30 years of construction experience to every job — from subfloor prep to finished trim, nothing gets skipped.

Every job is done by the same crew: James handles the install, Josh assists on larger jobs, and Christina brings an eye for design to help customers make the right material choices. You're not getting a random crew — you're getting us.
